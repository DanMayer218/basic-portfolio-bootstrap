DANIEL MAYER// DESIGNER AND DEVELOPER

Daniel Mayer is a New York city artist, designer and full stack developer. With a lifetime of practice and education in the fine arts, Daniel has proven himself to be a truly talented designer. Daniel showcased his design and strong leadership skills with his design, build and maintain company New Leaf Metropolitan Landscaping which he owned and operated for ten years. Catering to New York City's most elite clientele Daniel's standards were at the forefront of executing the vision for the client.

While Daniel has first hand experience as a front end developer building websites for his two companies and again for himself as an artist. He decided to formalize his coding knowlege by attending a coding bootcamp through Columbia University's Engineering department. Since graduation, Daniel has worked with Jose Rodriguez to design and implement user interfaces to enrich user experience for various clients websites. Currently, Daniel is working with John and David Kadish to catalog and create the official website for Philip Guston.

Daniels talents as an artist and designer, fluency as a coder, instincts as a leader, personability,understanding and communication with clientele, make him a fantastic addition to any team.
